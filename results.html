By Anja

After learning about different methods in class and evaluating the goal of our portfolio, we decided to use Python to optimize for a high Sharpe Ratio. Finding the highest Sharpe Ratio that our portfolio can have ensures that we are balancing risk and return. We didn't want to optimize for highest return because the goal of our portfolio is to be a safe fund for young adults starting their retirement funds. Having a super risky portfolio was not something we were lookin for. We decided to not optimize for a low risk because our target demographic is young adults, so they have a while before retirement and can handle some risk.So, optimizing for a high Sharpe Ratio ended up being the best way forward for us.
